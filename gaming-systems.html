The Pros and Cons

Which gaming system is right for you?

The Bottom Line

Each gaming system has strengths and weaknesses. The best choice depends on how and what you like to play.

PC Gaming

Pros

  • High performance
  • Customizable
  • Large game library

Cons

  • Expensive
  • Requires maintenance

PlayStation

Pros

  • Exclusive titles
  • Strong performance

Cons

  • Limited customization

Xbox

Pros

  • Game Pass
  • Cross-platform support

Cons

  • Fewer exclusives

Nintendo Switch

Pros

  • Portable
  • Family friendly

Cons

  • Lower performance
